About Qaroo

India's Most Loved
Learning Platform

Qaroo is India's largest ed-tech company and the creator of India's most loved school learning app. Offering highly personalised and effective learning programs for classes 1–12 and competitive exams like JEE, NEET, IAS and more.

Why Choose Us

Shaping the Future
of Student Learning

Learning is pivotal for a student's success in academics and life. The Digital Age is deeply shaping the way students learn and will also determine their future prospects. At Qaroo, we encourage students to embrace this fast-changing world and prepare them for tomorrow.

We craft learning journeys for every student that address their unique needs. We believe in the power of one-to-one learning that allows students to be holistically involved in their education and become active, lifelong learners.
